About RoboHack

AurinkoLab: RoboHack is a super intense 48-hour event bringing together young robot builders, designers, and tech lovers to innovate!

Participants will form teams to design, build, and program robots to solve real-world challenges. Whether you've built robots before or are totally new to it, dive into RoboHack to learn, work with others, and bring your ideas to life!
